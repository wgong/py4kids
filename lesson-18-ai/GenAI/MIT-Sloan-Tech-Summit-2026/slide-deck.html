Cerebras

Wafer-Scale Computing for AI

The world's largest chip, designed from the ground up for AI workloads.

Wafer-Scale Engine Prototype

My personal photo from the summit

Cerebras cerebras.ai

Wafer-Scale + OpenAI Partnership

Full Wafer Processing

Instead of cutting wafers into chips, Cerebras uses the entire wafer as one processor. 462mm of continuous compute.

Memory Architecture

900,000 cores with unified memory. Eliminates the memory bottleneck that plagues traditional GPU clusters.

OpenAI Collaboration

Partnership focuses on training speed and inference at scale. Dramatically faster iteration cycles for model development.
